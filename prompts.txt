2. Profitability Check (BEFORE ACCEPTING)

For each tender, compute:

Step A: Determine unwind direction

If institution SELLS to you → you are LONG → you must SELL to market

If institution BUYS from you → you are SHORT → you must BUY from market

Step B: Pull market data

GET /securities/book?ticker=...

GET /securities?ticker=...

Step C: Compute VWAP-based edge

Compute market VWAP from top levels of the order book on the unwind side

Compute:

edge_per_share =
  (market_VWAP - tender_price)   if LONG
  (tender_price - market_VWAP)   if SHORT

Step D: Subtract costs

Subtract trading fee (trading_fee from /securities)

Subtract a conservative slippage buffer

Step E: Liquidity constraint

Verify that available quantity on the unwind side ≥ tender quantity

If liquidity is insufficient → DO NOT ACCEPT

Step F: Acceptance rule

Accept the tender only if:

edge_per_share > minimum_profit_threshold

liquidity constraint is satisfied

portfolio limits will not be breached

3. Accepting the Tender

Use POST /tenders/{id} to accept

After acceptance:

Record:

position size

direction

ticker

acceptance time

4. Immediate Unwind (NO DELAY)

Once a tender is accepted:

Unwind immediately

Use:

MARKET orders or

MARKETABLE LIMIT orders

max_trade_size from /securities

If tender quantity > max per order:

Slice into multiple orders

Goal:
Minimize market risk exposure time.

5. Safety Logic
End-of-Simulation Protection

Use GET /case to monitor time remaining

If simulation is near the end:

Force-close all open positions at market

Cancel all open orders (POST /commands/cancel)
